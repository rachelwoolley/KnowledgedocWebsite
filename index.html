Knowledgedoc: Documentation Specialists

Knowledgedoc brings a new generation of technical writing expertise to the business and IT world. We have been successfully operating for 17 years and provide a quality, professional service that delivers information quickly and easily to person who needs it, when they need it.

Knowledgedoc works with all types of organisations in Australia to identify and capture critical business knowledge. We consistently deliver high quality single-sourced documentation solutions to satisfied clients - making critical knowledge readily available in a range of outputs.

Knowledgedoc specialises in producing high quality documentation for a variety of industries and any target audience. Knowledgedoc predominantly produces:

  • software documentation
  • policies and procedures
  • standard operating procedures
  • documentation of critical business knowledge.

Knowledgedoc also provides a number of related services for businesses, including:

  • business process analysis
  • business continuity plans
  • knowledge management audits.
