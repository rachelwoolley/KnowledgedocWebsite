How we deliver excellent documentation

To deliver excellent documentation to you, we use our documentation methodology, developed over many years, to:

  • fully understand your requirements by working closely with you
  • identify the best approach to achieve your organisation's unique needs by consulting with you and your stakeholders
  • agree project and quality deliverables so that every step of the project leads to a successful outcome
  • apply project management principles so that the project is managed successfully from start to finish
  • document and finalise the content using a collaborative approach with your subject matter experts
  • publish the content to the agreed outputs once the content is signed off
  • review with you the outcome to ensure that the project meets or even exceeds your expectations
  • arrange maintenance services so that that the success continues for the life of the documentation.
