Knowledgedoc’s Documentation Methodology

Over the past decade, KnowledgeDoc has developed a consistent professional approach to documentation projects. Like any project, a documentation project is a success when all parties understand and agree to the requirements, timelines and end goals.

The twelve steps outlined here have enabled KnowledgeDoc to deliver excellent value and quality for customers such as a major Australasian retailer, GlaxoSmithKline, SPC Ardmona, Computing Directions Australia and Monash University.

Knowledgedoc’s Twelve Stage Methodology Overview

  1. Initial meeting
  2. Initial scoping
  3. Proposal
  4. Collect all relevant existing documentation
  5. Detailed scoping and design
  6. Interview subject matter experts
  7. Write first draft
  8. Review cycle
  9. Sign off
  10. Publish
  11. Project review
  12. Ongoing maintenance
