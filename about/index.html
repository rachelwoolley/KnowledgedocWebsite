About Us

Knowledgedoc is a small business that focuses on providing technical writing services for other companies. Knowledgedoc was founded in 1997 as Monarch Computing Services. Monarch Computing services originally focused on writing documentation for IT companies. As we gained experience in a wider range of fields we changed our name to Knowledgedoc to better reflect the services we offer.

Knowledgedoc specialises in producing high quality documentation for a range of industries and target audiences. Our writers can understand the highly technical work done by your company and turn it into documentation your audience can easily understand.

Knowledgedoc also works to provide companies with business process analysis services and business continuity plans. All of our services centre around the idea of ensuring that your company's knowledge is preserved.

To find out more about Knowledgedoc read details of our recent customers, find out about our team or follow us on LinkedIn.
