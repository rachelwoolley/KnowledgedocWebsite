Internship Program

Knowledgedoc runs an internship program for students and graduates looking to enter the technical writing industry. The program gives students the opportunity to gain hands-on experience in a variety of software programs including:

  • Specialist technical writing software such as Author-it
  • Microsoft Office and the Microsoft Word Perfect-it add-in
  • Image editing programs such as HyperSnap
  • Video recording software such as Camtasia

Knowledgedoc's internship program gives students opportunities to practice the craft of technical writing as well as the opportunity to develop video procedures.

Contact us to apply or find out more about the internship program.
