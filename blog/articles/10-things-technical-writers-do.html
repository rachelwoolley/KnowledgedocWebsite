Ten things I bet you didn’t know a technical writer can do02/06/15

Technical writers don’t just write. And we don’t just write technical information. Here are some skills that many technical writers have that you might not be aware of:

  1. Visual and graphic design.
  2. Writing non-technical content.
  3. Creating presentations and videos.
  4. Creating websites (as well as writing content for them).
  5. Editing.
  6. Project management.
  7. Scoping documentation projects.
  8. Usability analysis.
  9. Product testing.
  10. Information design.
