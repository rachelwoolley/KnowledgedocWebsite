Semi-Serviced Offices to Rent in Wheelers Hill

We offer furnished one to two person offices for rent, located in Wheelers Hill, south-east Melbourne.

Rates are:

  • $160 a week for a one year lease
  • $170 a week for a six month lease
  • $180 a week for a one to five month lease. All figures exclude GST.

What's included in the rental:

  • Fully furnished one-person office. Includes desk, bookshelf hutch, full length lockable cupboard, three-drawer mobile pedestal including filing drawer. Can be set up to cater for two people if required.
  • Access to shared board room, meeting room, kitchen and lounge area on the same floor. There is a nominal charge of $10-15 per hour for the use of the meeting rooms.
  • Fast broadband internet access with fibre optic cable rated at 100 Mbs.
  • Phone line with your own number and voice mail message, if needed.
  • Electricity, rates and water.
  • Cleaning of common areas.
  • Tea and coffee.
  • Fair use of fax, laser printer and other equipment.
  • Shared locked post box.
  • 24 hour access to the building. However, there is restricted access outside normal business hours that requires your card key to access.
  • Plenty of car parking.

What's not included in the rental:

  • Cost of phone calls though they are cheap, high quality VOIP (voice over IP.
  • Large print and photocopier runs. These will be billed at cost.
  • Dedicated receptionist (you can easily divert to your voice mail or your mobile). Talk to us if you need additional services.
